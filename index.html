Lancers HQ


Welcome to Lancers HQ! We are a passionate small business based in Cebu, dedicated to bringing the excitement of Yu-Gi-Oh! to our community. Located near the University of Cebu, our main headquarters is where our love for the game comes to life.


At Lancers, we're more than just a store—we're a group of dedicated duelists who share a deep passion for Yu-Gi-Oh! Our journey started with a simple love for the game, and now we're here to share that enthusiasm with you. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting your dueling adventure, we've got the cards and expertise to help you build your dream deck.


Join us at Lancers, where the heart of Yu-Gi-Oh! beats strong. Let's duel!
